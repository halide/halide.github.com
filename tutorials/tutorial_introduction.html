Halide releases come with a set of tutorials in the form of commented source code. We recommend you learn Halide by reading, compiling, and running them, and by making your own modifications to them. Get your hands dirty! The tutorials can also be browsed here, using the links on the left. In their online form they contain figures and output snippets. For questions or comments, email halide-dev@lists.csail.mit.edu.
